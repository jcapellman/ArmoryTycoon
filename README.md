# Armory Tycoon
A simulation game where you're the CEO of an Armory startup company. From Day 1 to when you either retire or go bankrupt.

Until closer to 1.0, the UI/UX will be minimal to avoid re-work.

## Systems to be developed/designed for 0.1
* Randomized Events
* Design Phase
* Iteration of Pre Production Phase
* Release Phase (reviews, demand vs supply etc)
* Marketing Budgets
* Market Demand
* Bank Loans
* End of Game (Retirement/Bankruptcy)

### Additional Elements for 1.0
* Computer Competitors
* Nostaglia Releases
* Investing and buyout of competitors
* Contracts for money and popularity
* End of Year Awards
* Licensing of Designs
